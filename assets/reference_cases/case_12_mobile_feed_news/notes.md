# Case 12 - Mobile Feed/News Screens

## Source

Figma mobile wireframe reference, Feed & News page (`39:1113`).

## Useful Pattern

Feed/news screens rely on editorial hierarchy: featured story, article card, category rail, carousel, author row, date, body text, and related article list.

## Repair Intent

Protect title legibility and image-title pairing. Keep horizontal carousels intentionally wider than the viewport when they communicate swipe, but avoid accidental clipping of fixed content.

## Common Failures

- Hero title overlaps image or loses contrast area.
- Article card image, label, title, and date detach.
- Carousel peek becomes accidental crop.
- Long article body collides with author/action rows.
- Category controls become visually detached from lists.
