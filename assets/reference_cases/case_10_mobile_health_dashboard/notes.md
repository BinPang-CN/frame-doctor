# Case 10 - Mobile Health/Dashboard Screens

## Source

Figma mobile wireframe reference, Health page (`39:1118`).

## Useful Pattern

Health screens mix dashboard cards, service grids, article lists, doctor/result rows, activity charts, nutrition logs, and daily metric summaries.

## Repair Intent

Preserve metric-card comparability, chart label clearance, service-grid rhythm, and list row legibility. Health data needs calm hierarchy and strong spacing, because numbers and labels are safety-relevant.

## Common Failures

- Metric cards lose equal width or baseline alignment.
- Chart labels collide with bars, rings, or legends.
- Service icons and labels separate.
- Doctor/appointment rows lose avatar-name-meta-action pairing.
- Nutrition rows misalign calories, grams, and food names.
